BITS lab 2

  1. Is it a good idea to reuse password? Why?

    2. The more a password is reused, the more opportunities there are for that password to be compromised or stolen.

  2. Why should all passwords expire?

    3. Yes, the shorter the password expiration policy, the shorter their window to compromise systems and exfiltrate data.

  3. Why should we have minimum length for password?

    4. Short passwords can be easily broken with tools that do dictionary or brute force attacks against the passwords.

  4. Why should the lockout an account after a certain number of failed attempts.

    5. Brute force password attacks can be automated to try thousands or even millions of password combinations for any or all user accounts. Limiting the number of failed sign-ins that can be performed nearly eliminates the effectiveness of such attacks.

  5. Why should there be a 30 min waiting period before we are allowed to unlock the account?

    6. A denial-of-service (DoS) condition can be created if an attacker abuses the Account lockout threshold policy setting and repeatedly attempts to sign in with a specific account.
