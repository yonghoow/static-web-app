Justification for controls

We recommend using more money and human resources in implementing preventive controls to improve the security of the company. This strategy will deter most of the data breaches from happening at the first place.

On the event that a data breach can overcome the preventive strategy, there is still defective and corrective controls in place. However, our emphasis is still on prevention measures rather than damage control.

Conclusion

The loss or unauthorized disclosure of information in a data breach can lead to serious consequences and can result in substantial harm to individuals and reputation lost to the organization.

As prevention is better than cure, businesses and organiations should not neglect the cyber security aspect. Previous security breaches provide good learning opportunities to adopt the best security strategies, preventing future similar breaches and making it safer to navigate the online world.
